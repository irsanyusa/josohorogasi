Best Flower Delivery Services | TIME Stamped

A bouquet of flowers is a timeless way to bring joy and delight to any recipient, in any room and on any occasion. Whether you’re shopping for a partner, friend, family member or yourself, flowers are an easy way to show that you care, in a way that really packs a punch. With Valentine’s Day coming up, many couples will be eyeing up beautiful arrangements of roses and other romantic blooms, but a bouquet is just as welcome on Mother’s Day, birthdays—or just because.

But where should you buy the flowers? “If you’re looking for a bouquet or an arrangement, yes you can check out your deli, but first check your local florist and then the larger delivery services,” says Lindsay Jones, floral and event designer at Studio Jones in New York. If you don’t have a go-to local expert, the good news is that a gorgeous bunch of flowers is only an order away. The best flower delivery services take on the task of arranging, packaging and delivering your flowers so all you need to do is pick out the bouquet you think they’ll love most. Depending on your budget, the delivery window and the style of arrangement you’re looking for, there will be a flower delivery service for you that gets the right bouquet delivered on time.

To help you find the best bouquets for all your flower arrangement needs, we’ve compiled this list of the best flower delivery services for every occasion.

Our top picks for the best flower delivery service

Best flower delivery service for unique bouquet designs: Farmgirl Flowers

It can be easy to lose the personal touch when you’re operating on a national scale, but Farmgirl Flowers has retained its rustic, farm-fresh sensibility. Female-founded and predominantly female-run, the brand incorporates a mix of rare rose varieties and other unique flowers into each of their trendy, attractive designs. There is a big emphasis on seasonality and each bouquet is wrapped in upcycled burlap from coffee roasters, which enhances the artisanal aesthetic. You won’t find any brightly saturated hues here as everything leans towards a softer palette of complementary colors, but the resulting bouquets are very tasteful. A range of flowers are always available in the “free ship shop” for greater value, but there is no same-day or next-day delivery. Ultimately, if you are willing to pay a bit more for sustainable blooms and a farmhouse aesthetic, then we think you’ll be very happy with Farmgirl Flowers.

Specifications:

  • Shipping: No same-day or next-day delivery, some free shipping
  • Price range: $49+
  • Subscription option: Yes

What we like:

  • Tasteful arrangements of interesting, unique flowers
  • Packaging is attractive and sustainable
  • The Free Ship Shop ensures added value for a range of bouquets
  • Subscriptions are available starting at $59.99

What we don’t like:

  • No same-day or next-day delivery
  • Arrangements are limited to one specific aesthetic

The bottom line:

Farmgirl Flowers offer the ultimate arrangements of artisanal farmhouse blooms, with the attractive and eye-catching designs being worth the slightly higher prices.

Best flower delivery service for variety: The Bouqs Company

Whether you’re looking for a small yet vibrant arrangement or a deluxe extravaganza, The Bouqs Company has something for you. Not only do they offer a wide array of color schemes, flower types and arrangement styles, but every bouquet is carefully packaged and arrives in consistently great condition. The florists here use a range of interesting blooms that create very stylish, expensive-looking bouquets—there are no “filler flowers” here. Each arrangement is available in three sizes, so you can scale the bouquet to suit the occasion and your budget. Subscribers also benefit from 30% off many popular choices, with the ability to customize each delivery as much or as little as desired.

Same-day delivery is available for many options on the website, although these deliveries are fulfilled by local florists and may not be quite as perfect as The Bouqs Company’s own deliveries. Shipping prices are also not included in the listed price, so keep that in mind when deciding on your budget. However, we think the little bit extra you spend is very much worth it for the quality of flowers you get, time after time.

Specifications:

  • Shipping: Same-day and next-day available

What we like:

  • Flowers stay vibrant and fresh for much longer than other bouquets

Best flower service for local deliveries: Teleflora

Teleflora may be headquartered in Los Angeles, California but it counts over 10,000 member florists throughout the USA and Canada. What this means is that Teleflora is able to work with local flower services on each order, ensuring fresh blooms in great condition and fast shipping no matter where your gift recipient is based. There is even a global presence of 20,000 florists outside of North America, in case you’re sending flowers further afield. This is ideal if you live far away from the person you are sending flowers to and therefore don’t know the local delivery options; order from Teleflora and they’ll do the hard work for you! The majority of bouquets are delivered same-day and come in a vase, for enhanced presentation and practicality. In terms of design, these arrangements skew traditional, in bright colors or classic reds and with lots of roses and lilies. You won’t get the most exciting or original designs, but prices are affordable, service is warm and delivery is fast.

Specifications:

  • Shipping: Mostly same-day shipping
  • Price range: $30+
  • Subscription option: No

What we like:

  • Local florist partnerships throughout North America for fresh, high-quality flowers
  • Same-day delivery is the norm, not a special exception
  • Prices are affordable
  • Lots of color variation and fun themed vases

What we don’t like:

  • Bouquets are a little unoriginal in design

Support local businesses through Teleflora’s giant florist network, ensuring fresh blooms and fast delivery no matter where you’re sending flowers to.

How to keep flowers fresh

Your beautiful bouquet sadly won’t last forever, but there are several steps you can take to help preserve its freshness as long as possible. The most important ingredient? Water. “Fresh water daily,” recommends Jones. “Tip out the water from the vase your flowers are in, add water, almost let it overflow. That’s a great way to make sure you’re really cleaning out the water.” Cutting an inch off the bottom of the stems will help the flowers drink more efficiently, but the fresh water is the key ingredient.

You also want to avoid any extreme conditions, whether that’s heat, cold or direct sunlight. “Flowers definitely wilt if you keep them in that pretty window or that sunny spot on your dining room table,” she warns. You can still place them in that spot for part of the day, but be sure to move the vase into a shadier area when you’re not directly enjoying them. And don’t worry if you accidentally toss out the small packet of plant food that may be included in delivery; Jones says they can be beneficial but they definitely aren’t necessary for healthy blooms.

Overall quality

Flowers are individual living things, which means there will always be a level of variation in each bouquet ordered. However, consistency in quality and general look are always important, so that customers can feel confident in what they’re purchasing. I also assessed services on their pricing, range of styles available, shipping services, type of flower used and ability to provide gift add-ons.

Customer service and satisfaction

Not everyone looks for the same thing in a bouquet of flowers, so it was important to consider the perspectives of a range of potential buyers. I took into account my own experience ordering flowers and also consulted floral and event designer Lindsay Jones of Studio Jones on flower delivery, flower care and what to look for when ordering a bouquet. Finally, I incorporated the reviews of customers and other industry experts, to ensure each service on this list stood up to consistent scrutiny.

How to find the best flower delivery service

Consider average bouquet costs

Flower arrangements come in a range of sizes and prices, from around $30 to well over $100. Depending on the occasion and your budget, you likely have an idea of how much you’d like to spend so keep that in mind when considering which service to use. Some vendors specialize in specialty blooms or more complex arrangements, while others offer bouquets at a range of price levels. Larger displays will usually cost more, while some services may offer sale pricing or holiday pricing around popular flower gifting occasions. Then, there are subscription services (similar to meal delivery services or beauty subscription boxes, where you select a regularly occurring delivery option). If you’re looking to buy a subscription as opposed to a one-off bouquet, then you may be able to receive discounted pricing, especially for longer subscription periods such as 12 months.

Turnaround time

If you’re ordering flowers to arrive on a major holiday, such as Valentine’s Day or Mother’s Day, then you will want to order early in advance if possible to guarantee delivery on time. Even if services normally offer quick shipping, the big holidays can be so busy that regular delivery windows are not possible, so plan in advance if possible. (Same advice goes for online framing services, which also increase in demand close to major holidays.)

If you are spontaneously buying flowers and want them delivered as quickly as possible, don’t fret: There are several delivery services that specialize in same-day or next-day delivery. However, not everyone is able to accommodate those delivery windows so make sure to check the shipping options before you commit to a bouquet. Choosing local providers that will deliver from within a nearby radius is one way to increase the chance of fast delivery.

Variety of arrangements available

Depending on the occasion, your choice of bouquet may vary significantly. If you’re celebrating a milestone birthday or anniversary, you may want to go with a large statement bouquet or a bespoke arrangement from a more high-end service. “I look at their other work and look for a diversity of style and product,” says Jones, “because then I know I’m going to get something that has been thought through and not just the same-old, day-in-day-out design.” For a smaller occasion or a “just because” gift, you may want to opt for something more informal and vibrant. Some services also allow you to include additional gifts with your bouquet, such as chocolates or wine, if that matches the sentiment you’re going for.

Lastly, consider who you’re buying the flowers for and what they like. “When picking a bouquet, you really need to go for the ones that will bring the person you’re buying for the most joy,” says Jones. Are they a big fan of bright colors or do they prefer a more minimalist design? Do they favor classic flowers like roses and lilies, or do they like more unusual blooms? Most flower delivery services will feature a range of different styles, but some specialize in particular aesthetics or flower types which may suit your recipient the best. Perhaps their favorite gifts aren’t flowers at all, but instead feature tropical plants or succulents. Whatever their preference, there’s a flower delivery service for them.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

How much is flower delivery?

Flower delivery services will vary in price depending on a few key factors, such as the type of flowers you’re buying, the size of the bouquet and the speed of delivery. Rush orders are likely to cost more for same-day or next-day shipping, although some delivery services include this shipping in the sticker price. High-end florist services that use sustainably grown blooms or more complex designs will likely price their bouquets higher than mid-level services. This list includes floral delivery options for as low as $30 per bouquet, while some of the more luxurious arrangements can go for hundreds of dollars, especially if they are preserved for longevity.

Which flower delivery service is cheapest?

There are a number of affordably priced flower services on this list which include bouquets in the $30-50 range. If you are a Prime member, then the free shipping included with Amazon’s Benchmark Bouquet service may make that the cheapest option for you. 1-800-Flowers is reliably competitive with its pricing, as are From You Flowers and FTD Flowers. If you know that you are going to be ordering bouquets on a regular basis, then a subscription is a great way to save money, such as those offered by BloomsyBox and The Bouqs Company.

Also, consider the kind of flowers you’re buying if you want to keep things affordable. Jones recommends roses as a classic choice that is often found at a more friendly price point, but also favors another classic: daisies. “Daisies last a long time and they’re really good because people always know them,” she says.

What is the best international flower delivery?

The majority of flower services on the list focus on the contiguous US, but Teleflora is well regarded for its extensive network of member florists outside of North America. With 20,000 different member florists to partner with, Teleflora is a great option to consider for international flower delivery. Unfortunately, Amazon does not currently offer its Benchmark Bouquets service internationally.

How much should I tip a flower delivery person?

Most delivery services will include a delivery fee in their pricing, so tips are not required—but a little can still go a long way if your bouquet is being hand-delivered. Jones explains that there isn’t a set industry standard but recommends giving somewhere in between what you’d tip on a cup of coffee and what you’d tip movers. Also, consider the circumstances of delivery, such as if it’s Valentine’s Day or if the weather is very cold; these jobs will warrant a better tip.

What is the best app to order flowers from?

All of the flower delivery services on our list are available online, with comprehensive and easy to navigate websites that are also accessible on mobile. The majority do not have dedicated smartphone applications, but 1-800-Flowers does have a popular app that has an average rating of 4.6 with 27,000 votes. This is a convenient way to order flowers for a loved one while on the go. While you can also order flowers for delivery through the popular delivery app DoorDash, we would recommend going to a dedicated flower delivery service for higher quality bouquets and a greater selection of blooms.
